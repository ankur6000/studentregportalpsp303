ℹ️ How Transport Works
• Upon approval, admin assigns your Bus No., Seat No. and Route
• Female seats are grouped: seat 1F assigned Female → next female gets 2F, etc.
• Seats are periodically reshuffled (scheduled by admin) while preserving gender grouping
• Students with Attendance >90% AND Academic % >90% may request a seat change from their dashboard
• If your route is full, a new bus is automatically added
